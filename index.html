Accelerator Delivery Process v1.0

Owner: Mentor Operations Team | Status: Execution

Process Objective

To efficiently source, contract, manage, and track the delivery of purchased Accelerator Services (Tech Talks, Office Hours, Deep Dives, 1:1 Sessions). This optimized process ensures accurate financial traceability and strictly adheres to the new Service Level Agreements (SLAs).

Visualizing the commitment for each phase. The optimized process establishes a clear 8 Business Day timeline for SOW creation and release (Phases 4-6).

Phase 2: Planning & Request

Optimization Focus: Finance Traceability

To prevent blocks in Phase 5, you must link the delivery request to the original financial record.

  • ! MANDATORY: Provide direct link to original Deal Ticket (JIRA/CRM).
  • ✓ Specify Total Units Purchased.
  • ✓ Specify Proposed Delivery Services.
SLA Commitment: 2 Business Days (Planning to Ticket)

Key Communications

You will be informed of the new 8 Business Day SLA for SOW generation during initial training. Traceability is your primary contribution to speed.

Accelerator Delivery Team: Phase 3 & 4

As the Accelerator Delivery team, you are the owner of the SOW Request Checklist. If this is incomplete, the 5-day Mentor Success / Legal SLA does not begin.

Phase 4: Mandatory SOW Readiness Checklist Interactive Tool

Check off items as you gather them from the SME/MOPA ticket. All items are required for submission.

Select all items to verify readiness.

Phase 5: Review & Approval (Managed by Mentor Success)

The Gating Item

Finance verification of the Deal Ticket Link and available budget is the strict prerequisite. The Mentor Success team manages the full 5-day SLA timeline, coordinating between Finance and Legal.

Joint SLA: 5 Business Days (Review + Draft)

Phase 8: Accrual & Closure

This phase is owned by the Accelerator Delivery team (working with Finance).

  • 1. Verify "Units Remaining" tally in the MOPA Tracker.
  • 2. Perform Monthly Reconciliation within 5 Business Days.
